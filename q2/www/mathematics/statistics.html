Course Description

Introduction of statistics is a branch of mathematics that is exploring and dealing
with the collection data analysis, bias and the mean squared error of estimates;
simple ANOVA and hypothesis testing. Statistics is the presentation of
masses by numerical data through the introduction of experimental design
and the analysis of variance.

Florence Nightingale

Nightingale was a social reformer and statistician and was the founder of modern
nursing. Nightingale did not only give nursing a reputation but she also laid the
foundation for professional nursing at St. Thomas Hospital, which was the first
every nursing school in the world. We now recognize a special day called
“International Nurses day” which is celebrated on Nightingale birthday.
Nightingale social reforms helped get rid of prostitution laws.
