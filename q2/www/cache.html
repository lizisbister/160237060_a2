What is Browser Cache?

A browser cache is used when someone is clearing a website with no confusion. After visiting a website, your browser will “absorb” or take pieces of information from that website and will place that information into storage that is saved onto your computer’s hard drive. The reason why clearing your cache or cookies is a must to reset MailChimp, is because MailChimp uses browser cache to know how many times a user visits the website, which will allow viewers to only see the pop-up form once and once the viewers see the form, it will not be displayed again. Implying that if you clear your cache, the browser will reset the website memory and all the information that was saved, is deleted and the pop-up form on MailChimp will reset as if you are seeing the form for the first time again.
