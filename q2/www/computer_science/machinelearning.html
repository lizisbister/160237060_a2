Ada Lovelace

Ada Lovelace proposed mechanical general-purpose computer. Lovelace was the
first person to understand that machines had applications that went beyond
the normal calculation. Where she than created the first algorithm that was designed
to carry out by a machine and then went on to be known as one of the very first computer
programmers. Lovelace went beyond the standard computer programmers and saw
that computer had more than just the standard capability of calculations and number
crunching.
