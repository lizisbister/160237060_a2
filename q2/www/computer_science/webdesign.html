Alan Turing

Specializes in computer science and had a significant role in influencing the
development of theoretical computer science by providing the algorithm and
commutating with the Turing Machine. Which is now known as the general
model of the general-purpose computer. With his superior knowledge in the
computer science world, Alan Turing is now known as the father of theoretical
computer science. Turing previously worked at National physical laboratory and
designed the Automatic Computing Engine and help developed the Manchester
Computers.
